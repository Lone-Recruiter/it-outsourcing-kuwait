Senior IBM DB2 on z/OS Specialist

Status: Must be proficient at both Developing and Administrating

Position Overview

We are seeking a Senior IBM DB2 on z/OS Specialist to manage, maintain, and support enterprise DB2 environments on IBM Mainframe platforms. Required Experience

  • 15+ Years’ experience in Database Administration (DB2)
  • Experience with IBM Mainframe (CICS, MQ, QMF, Data Rep for DB2, DB2 Connect and DB2 Admin)
  • Experience with DB2 Recovery, DB2 Log Analysis, and DB2 Utilities
  • Experience planning, installing, configuring, customizing, testing, administering, maintaining, upgrading, and implementing DB2 for z/OS
  • Experience maintaining and troubleshooting DB2
  • Experience performing Backup and Recovery, including disaster recovery
  • Experience developing policies and procedures to ensure integrity of database environments
  • Experience designing automation routines to perform DBA tasks
  • Experience assisting developers with database development tasks
  • Experience building infrastructure and developing solutions to convert existing systems to web-enabled systems
  • Experience training junior and entry-level employees
  • Documentation skills to document tasks, procedures, and responsibilities

Certifications

  • IBM or DB2 related certifications a plus
