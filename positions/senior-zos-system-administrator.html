Senior z/OS System Administrator

Salary: 2,250 KWD / Month

Location: Kuwait

Status: Position will be available in about 3 months (End of September, beginning of October)

Position Overview

We are looking for a highly skilled IBM z/OS Senior System Administrator to join our IT team. The successful candidate will be responsible for the installation, configuration, maintenance, and overall management of IBM z/OS systems, ensuring high levels of system performance, availability, and security. The role will support and optimize the mainframe environment and business-critical applications running on z/OS.

Required Experience

  • Minimum 5+ years of hands-on experience as a z/OS System Administrator or similar role.
  • At least 3+ years of experience in a senior or lead capacity.
  • Strong experience with IBM z/OS system software and components such as JES2/JES3, TSO, SMP/E, and related utilities.
  • Experience installing, configuring, maintaining, patching, and optimizing IBM z/OS environments.
  • Strong knowledge of z/OS system architecture, utilities, performance tuning, and capacity management.
  • Experience with mainframe security tools such as RACF, ACF2, or Top Secret.
  • Experience managing system resources including CPU, memory, DASD, tape, zFS, and related mainframe storage components.
  • Knowledge of mainframe networking concepts including TCP/IP, VTAM, and SNA.
  • Hands-on experience with performance tools such as RMF, SMF, and z/OS Performance Toolkit.
  • Experience with backup, recovery, disaster recovery planning, DFSMS, FlashCopy, and related backup tools.
  • Proficiency in REXX, CLIST, or similar mainframe scripting languages.
  • Ability to automate system administration tasks, reporting, monitoring, and routine maintenance.
  • Strong troubleshooting, diagnostic, root-cause analysis, and problem management skills.
  • Experience documenting system configurations, procedures, troubleshooting steps, and environment changes.
  • Ability to collaborate with application teams, developers, IT teams, and management.
  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or a related field, or equivalent work experience.

Certifications

  • IBM z/OS-related certifications are preferred.
  • Mainframe security certifications related to RACF, ACF2, or Top Secret are an advantage.
  • Certifications in automation tools such as Tivoli Workload Scheduler or Control-M are preferred.
  • Additional certifications related to IBM Z, z/VM, mainframe operations, or hybrid cloud are an advantage.
