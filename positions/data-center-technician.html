Data Center Technician

Salary: 550 KWD / Month

Location: Kuwait

Notes: Shift Based (24/7). This is an RFP Tender, so the contract has not been accepted yet. If accepted, those whos CVs fit the requirements will be prioritized

Position Overview

Support the daily operation and maintenance of the organization's Data Center environment. The successful candidate will assist with IT infrastructure, server room operations, monitoring systems, and facility support to ensure high availability, reliability, and continuous operation of business-critical systems.

Required Experience

  • 2–3 years of experience in Data Center or IT Infrastructure support.
  • Knowledge of Data Center operations and server room best practices.
  • Experience with rack installation, cabling, and basic hardware support.
  • Knowledge of Windows Desktop support.
  • Experience with Active Directory.
  • Experience with backup systems.
  • Familiarity with IP Monitoring Tools.
  • Knowledge of Building Management Systems (BMS).
  • Experience with DCIM (Data Center Infrastructure Management).
  • Experience with Network Management Systems (NMS).
  • Basic understanding of facility infrastructure, including power and cooling systems.
  • Good troubleshooting and communication skills.

Certifications

  • A degree or diploma in Information Technology, Computer Science, Computer Engineering, or a related technical field is preferred.
  • Relevant Data Center, Microsoft, or infrastructure-related certifications are an advantage.
