Junior z/OS System Administrator

Salary: 1,800 KWD / Month

Location: Kuwait

Status: Position will be available in about 3 months (End of September, beginning of October) but has a chance to begin early depending on client's wishes

Position Overview

We are looking for a highly skilled IBM z/OS Senior System Administrator to join our IT team. The successful candidate will be responsible for the installation, configuration, maintenance, and overall management of IBM z/OS systems, ensuring high levels of system performance, availability, and security. The role will support and optimize the mainframe environment and business-critical applications running on z/OS.

Required Experience

  • Good experience with IBM z/OS system software and components such as JES2/JES3, TSO, SMP/E, and related utilities.
  • Experience installing, configuring, maintaining, patching, and supporting IBM z/OS environments.
  • Good understanding of z/OS system architecture, utilities, performance monitoring, and capacity planning.
  • Experience administering mainframe security using RACF, ACF2, or Top Secret.
  • Experience managing system resources including CPU, memory, DASD, tape, zFS, and related mainframe storage components.
  • Working knowledge of mainframe networking concepts including TCP/IP, VTAM, and SNA.
  • Experience using performance monitoring tools such as RMF, SMF, and z/OS Performance Toolkit.
  • Experience with backup, recovery, DFSMS, FlashCopy, and disaster recovery procedures.
  • Proficiency in REXX, CLIST, or similar mainframe scripting languages.
  • Ability to automate routine system administration, monitoring, reporting, and maintenance tasks.
  • Strong troubleshooting, diagnostic, and problem-solving skills with the ability to resolve day-to-day system issues.
  • Experience documenting system configurations, operational procedures, troubleshooting activities, and environment changes.
  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or a related field, or equivalent work experience.

Good to have Certifications

  • IBM z/OS-related certifications are preferred.
  • Mainframe security certifications related to RACF, ACF2, or Top Secret are an advantage.
  • Certifications in automation tools such as Tivoli Workload Scheduler or Control-M are preferred.
  • Additional certifications related to IBM Z, z/VM, mainframe operations, or hybrid cloud are an advantage.
