Data Center Technician Team Leader

Salary: 700 KWD / Month

Location: Kuwait

Notes: Shift Based (24/7)

Position Overview

Lead and supervise the daily operations of the Data Center team while ensuring the availability, reliability, and security of business-critical infrastructure. The successful candidate will coordinate technical activities, oversee server room operations, monitor facility systems, and provide guidance to Data Center Technicians while ensuring operational procedures and service levels are maintained.

Required Experience

  • 3–5 years of experience in Data Center or IT Infrastructure support.
  • Experience leading a small team or acting as a senior point of contact.
  • Strong knowledge of Data Center operations and server room best practices.
  • Experience with rack installation, cabling, and hardware support.
  • Knowledge of Windows Desktop support.
  • Experience with Active Directory.
  • Experience with backup systems.
  • Familiarity with IP Monitoring Tools.
  • Knowledge of Building Management Systems (BMS).
  • Experience with DCIM (Data Center Infrastructure Management).
  • Experience with Network Management Systems (NMS).
  • Basic understanding of facility infrastructure, including power and cooling systems.
  • Experience coordinating incidents, troubleshooting, and escalation.
  • Good communication, leadership, and organizational skills.

Certifications

  • A degree or diploma in Information Technology, Computer Science, Computer Engineering, or a related technical field is preferred.
  • Relevant Data Center, Microsoft, or infrastructure-related certifications are an advantage.
